---
name: grill-me
description: Interview the user relentlessly about a plan or design until reaching shared understanding, resolving each branch of the decision tree. Use for a lightweight, doc-free stress-test when user wants to be grilled on a plan or design or mentions "grill me". Do not use when the plan should be checked against the project's existing documentation, glossary, or ADRs, or when terminology/ADRs should be updated inline — use grill-with-docs for that.
---

Interview me relentlessly about every aspect of this plan until we reach a shared understanding. Walk down each branch of the design tree, resolving dependencies between decisions one-by-one. For each question, provide your recommended answer.

Ask the questions one at a time.

If a question can be answered by exploring the codebase, explore the codebase instead.
